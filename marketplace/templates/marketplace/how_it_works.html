Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know.

Is it free to post a task?
Yes — posting a task is completely free. You only pay the local professional once the job is done.
How does the rating system work?
Clients rate local professionals on 6 public criteria: Reliability & Punctuality, Quote / Price Accuracy, Value for Money, Service Quality / Workmanship, Communication & After-Service, and Timeline / Schedule Delivery.
What towns are covered?
Currently Suva, Nadi, Lautoka, Nausori, and Labasa. We're expanding across Fiji as our local professional network grows.
How much does it cost for local professionals?
Joining is free. Check the current pricing page for any service fees on completed jobs.
Are prices in Fijian Dollars?
Yes — all budgets, quotes, and payments on the Coconut Wireless Network are in FJD (Fijian Dollars).
What is the Market?
The Market is where clients and local professionals can list food, clothes, furniture, tools and other items for sale — separate from posting or quoting on jobs. Buyers order directly from a listing; sellers choose pickup or delivery, set their available dates, and decide whether orders need their approval or are auto-accepted.
Who can sell on the Market?
Any registered client or local professional account can post a Market listing — you don't need to be a verified tradie to sell items or food.
