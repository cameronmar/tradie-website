Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know.

Is it free to post a task?
Yes — posting a task is completely free. You only pay the local pro once the job is done.
How does the rating system work?
Clients rate local pros on 6 public criteria: Reliability & Punctuality, Quote / Price Accuracy, Value for Money, Service Quality / Workmanship, Communication & After-Service, and Timeline / Schedule Delivery. Private feedback about clients is still recorded separately for admin use only.
What towns are covered?
Currently Suva, Nadi, Lautoka, Nausori, and Labasa. We're expanding across Fiji as our local pro network grows.
How much does it cost for local pros?
Joining is free. Check the current pricing page for any service fees on completed jobs.
Are prices in Fijian Dollars?
Yes — all budgets, quotes, and payments on Coconut Wireless are in FJD (Fijian Dollars).
