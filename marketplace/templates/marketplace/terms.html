5. Platform Circumvention

Users must not knowingly or intentionally avoid Coconut Wireless fees, invoices, charges, or obligations after being introduced through the Platform.

5.1 What constitutes Platform Circumvention

Platform Circumvention includes:

  1. Exchanging contact details through Coconut Wireless and then arranging work outside the Platform;
  2. Cancelling, removing, or closing a task and later engaging directly for the same or similar work;
  3. Using Platform information to avoid fees;
  4. Encouraging another user to avoid Coconut Wireless fees.

5.2 Platform Circumvention Fee

If Coconut Wireless reasonably determines that Platform Circumvention occurred within twelve (12) months of introduction through the Platform, Coconut Wireless may charge a Platform Circumvention Fee.

The Platform Circumvention Fee is:

  1. Five percent (5%) of the total value of the services performed or contracted; or
  2. FJD $50.00,

whichever is greater. The fee is payable separately by both the Client and the Local Pro.

5.3 Evidence and determination

Coconut Wireless may rely on Platform messages, user admissions, task history, quotes, invoices, account activity, documents, or other reasonably available information when determining whether Platform Circumvention has occurred.

5.4 Enforcement

Coconut Wireless may suspend accounts, restrict quoting privileges, terminate accounts, recover unpaid fees as a debt, and recover reasonable legal or collection costs where Platform Circumvention is found to have occurred.
